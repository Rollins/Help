System Monitoring

Use this topic to learn about the tools and processes used to monitor the up-time, down-time, and response time of our websites and systems.

Pingdom

Rollins websites are monitored by Pingdom. The service pings each site every five minutes. If the site fails to respond, Pingdom notifies the Marketing Technology team. Some sites are also monitored for specific page strings, such as the page title. If those strings cannot be located, Pingdom notifies the team.

Pingdom also provides performance reports on these websites.


Google Analytics

Google Analytics monitors site traffic. Alerts are in place that notify the Marketing team if site traffic falls below a specified threshold. Google Analytics also provides a response time report.

 

Website Health Monitoring

The ASP.Net Health Monitoring service monitors most of our websites and reports user-facing errors. This service notifies the Marketing Technology team othe site performance, assists the team in diagnosing problems, and logs both errors and benign events.

Troubleshooting

Common troubleshooting procedures can help diagnose and resolve many website issues, such as site refresh problems and network connectivity errors.
