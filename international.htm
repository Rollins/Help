About International Franchisee Websites

Rollins provides a basic website to international franchisees. This website is based on a template reflecting Orkin brand standards and best practices.
The new franchisee completes the Website Design Scope of Work document to begin the process, and supplies specific required content to localize the website for the region.

Orkin international websites contain the following pages:

  • Homepage
  • About Us
  • Residential Pest Control
  • Termite Control, if the franchise offers this service
  • Commercial Pest Control
  • Pest Library
  • Contact Us

Before You Begin

A standard franchise website is developed in WordPress using an existing template. The franchisee specifies an Orkin Man image, provides one custom image for their website, and provides custom content to replace specific standard content.

Franchisees can request additional website customizations for an additional fee. These customizations include:

  • Standard template with custom English content
  • Standard Template with custom Other Language content
  • Custom images
  • Additional pages and content
  • Custom dynamic scripts or code
  • Hosting on a different web app server environment or CMS

Before beginning development, ensure you have the following informatino from the Marketing Manager:

  • Country
  • Domain verification
  • Contact Name
  • Email address for display on website
  • Email address for lead information
  • Telephone number
  • List of franchise services
  • Orkin Man image requested by franchisee
  • Custom images submitted by franchisee
  • Custom content submitted by franchisee

 

Verifying a Domain

Typically, domain verification and acquisition is handled by the International Franchise Team.

To verify ownership of a domain, navigate to https://www.whois.net/.
Type the franchise domain name in the search box and click the magnifying glass icon.
A registered domain returns information about the registrant, contacts, and name servers associated with the domain.
An unregistered domain returns a message stating that no data is available for that domain.

  • If the desired domain is already registered to Rollins, the Marketing Technology team maps the host name to the DNS.
  • If the desired domain is unregistered, the International Franchise team purchases the domain. The Marketing Technology team maps the host name to the DNS.
  • If the desired domain already registered to another party, send this information to the International Franchise representatives for Legal and IT Architecture, who will address the issue and advise the International Franchise team on next steps.

Quality Checking the International Website

Testing of the International Franchise website should be performed at three critical stages:

  • By the Developer during a self-review
  • By a peer during a peer review
  • By a QA analyst during the quality assurance phase

Subsequent testing of the website should be performed by:

  • The Business Owners prior to submitting the website to the franchisee for approval
  • The franchisee during review for approval

 

Follow these guidelines when performing quality assurance testing:

 

Orkininternational.com

  • The website is listed on Orkininternational.com in the appropriate section of the franchise list.
  • The pin representing the franchise exists on the map in the correct geographical region.
  • Clicking the link in the franchise list and clicking the associated map pin launches the expected informational pop-up window for the franchise. The pop-up window has the expected look and feel.
  • The pop-up window contains all appropriate contact information.
  • The contact information exactly matches the information submitted by the franchisee. All information is spelled correctly and number formatting exactly matches the franchisee’s submission.
  • The Contact Name is hyperlinked and directs to the franchise website’s Contact Us page.
  • The URL for the franchise website is correctly spelled and formatted and directs to the new franchise website.
  • All hyperlinks open in a new window.

Design and Layout

  • The red used on the site is #CC092F.
  • The fonts displayed on the site render as expected and match the standard template.
  • The site design and layout matches the standard template.
  • The page is responsive when the browser window is resized.
  • The site is tested on all applicable browsers and versions, including Safari.
  • The site is tested on all applicable operating systems and versions, including MacOS.
  • The site is tested on all applicable mobile platforms and versions.
  • The Orkin logo in the top left is hyperlinked and directs to the Home page.
  • The copyright in the site footer reflects the franchise name and the current year;
    for example © Orkin Hong Kong , LLC 2016.
    It does NOT read  © International Franches [sic] template , LLC 2016.
  • A browser page search for the following exact terms was performed and no results returned. A visual scan was also performed. This search was performed on each page of the site.
    • (country name)
    • (Franchise Name)
    • (link to commercial page)
    • (link to residential page)
  • The site’s search function was tested by entering known terms such as rodent, infest, flies and those search parameters returned expected results.
  • Entering random terms such as balloon, financial, batman returned no results.
  • Entering terms associated with services NOT offered by the franchise; for example, termite, retail, returned no results.
  • Each item in the navigation bar directs to the expected page.
  • Pages that are specific to services that the franchise does NOT offer should not display and should be unavailable in search results.

Home Page

  • The Orkin Man banner matches the image selected by the franchisee.
  • The Orkin International graphic is present in the right pane. This image is hyperlinked and directs to orkininternational.com. This link opens in a new window.
  • Any local, regional image submitted by the franchisee is present and captioned as specified. The image is scaled and cropped effective and is of high quality.
  • Custom content submitted by the franchisee is properly placed and formatted. This content matches exactly to the submitted content.
  • Custom content is checked for grammar and spelling and errors or suspected errors are flagged and submitted to the franchisee.
  • The contact phone number matches the number submitted by the franchisee. It is formatted as specified by the franchisee. This is tested on the splash header, Free Estimate banner, and Schedule Service Now pane.
  • Entering a first and last name and clicking Get Started in the Get Free Estimate banner directs the user to the Contact Us page.
  • Each URL on the Home page directs to the expected page. 
  • There are no broken images on this page.
